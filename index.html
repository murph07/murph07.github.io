First 90 Days

Three moves I would
explore from day one.

Hypothesis-driven, company-specific, realistic. Not a plan — a starting conversation.

01
SEO · Backlinks

Map the partner-driven keyword gap and build the first 5 venue guides.

Most corporate event search intent is location-and-format specific: "seminar venue countryside near Paris," "team-building space Lyon rooftop." Naboo's Hosts hold the content — the stories, the photos, the capacity data. I would audit which high-intent queries have no owned content, identify 5–10 Host partners with strong differentiation, and co-produce SEO venue guides that generate qualified organic traffic and earn natural backlinks.

→ Expected output: first backlinks live within 60 days, partner pages ranking within 90.

02
LinkedIn · Host Branding

Launch a Host spotlight series on LinkedIn — one story per week.

Venue owners and activity providers have audiences too. A well-produced spotlight post (photo, story, 3 key differentiators) tags the Host's own LinkedIn page, reaches their network, and positions Naboo as a platform that actively promotes its partners — not just lists them. This earns trust with existing Hosts and signals real value to prospective ones. I would build an editorial template, define the selection criteria, and coordinate with whichever photo/video resources are available.

→ Expected output: consistent weekly cadence established, measurable reach growth on Host content within 45 days.

03
CRM · Retention

Audit the onboarding flow and redesign the Host newsletter from the first issue.

Onboarding is where churn is decided — not at renewal. I would map the current Host journey from sign-up to first booking, identify drop-off points, and redesign the email sequence to include clear milestones, early-win prompts, and value reinforcement at key moments. In parallel, the Host newsletter should read like an insider brief — booking trends, platform updates, and specific tips for maximising their Naboo profile — not a corporate bulletin.

→ Expected output: redesigned onboarding flow live within 60 days, newsletter rhythm established at month 2.
